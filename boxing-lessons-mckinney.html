Why McKinney Residents Train With Us

Real Boxing, Close to Home

McKinney has exploded in growth over the past decade, but the boxing training options haven’t caught up. If you’re serious about learning real boxing — not a watered-down cardio class — the short drive south to Plano is worth every minute. Empire Boxing DFW is different.

Empire Boxing DFW at 1817 Stockton Trail in Plano is about 18–25 minutes from McKinney via US-75 south. Several of our current fighters make this drive regularly because they know they can’t get this level of private, one-on-one coaching from a Texas State Champion closer to home. Coach Francisco works with McKinney residents of all ages and levels — from kids just starting out to adults preparing for competition.

Coach Francisco Rodriguez is a Texas Cruiserweight State Champion and USA Boxing Certified trainer with over 30 years of professional boxing experience. Every session is private. You’re not sharing a trainer with 20 other people. From the moment you walk in, the focus is entirely on you — your stance, your combinations, your progress.

We train youth, adults, beginners, fitness clients, competitive boxers, and MMA fighters. All equipment is provided. We’re open 7 days a week with flexible scheduling.

Getting Here from McKinney

Empire Boxing DFW is located at 1817 Stockton Trail, Plano, TX 75023. From most parts of McKinney, it’s a 18–25 minutes drive.

From McKinney via US-75: Take US-75 (Central Expressway) south through Allen into Plano. Exit at Spring Creek Parkway, head west, then north on Custer Road to Stockton Trail.

From McKinney via Custer Road: Take Custer Road south directly through Allen and into Plano. Continue south past Spring Creek Parkway to Stockton Trail.
