Championship Victory

I started boxing at 15 years old with a dream, but it wasn't until I met the legendary John "Lawman" Lawson that I truly learned what it meant to be a fighter. Under his guidance, I developed not just skill, but discipline, heart, and respect for the craft.


I fought as an amateur, turned professional in 2005, and experienced the highs of victory. When life pulled me away from the ring, I never walked away from boxing — I continued to train, to learn, and to grow. Fifteen years later, during one of the toughest seasons of my life, I returned to the sport that built me. Boxing became my therapy, my strength, and my comeback.


Today, as the Texas Cruiserweight Champion, defeating fighters half my age, I stand as proof that true skill, dedication, and technique outlast everything. Boxing isn't about swinging wild — it's about precision, intelligence, and heart.


I'm here to teach you the right way to fight, the right way to train, and the right way to believe in yourself again.

About Coach Francisco

30+ Years of
Boxing Excellence

Texas Cruiserweight State Champion. USA Boxing Certified Coach. World Class Trainer — Making Elite Champions. From beginners to pros, kids of all ages — everyone trains the real way.

“My name is Francisco. I've been boxing for over 30 years. I have fought amateurs and professionally. I have trained professionals and now I am offering boxing lessons to anyone wanting to learn how to box or just have a great workout.”


I work with beginners to pros, kids of all ages, male and female — from competitive boxers and NFL athletes to the regular Joes looking to get in shape. MMA fighters and martial artists are welcome to sharpen their boxing skills. We provide all equipment in our private location.


We also offer strength training, conditioning & personal training. Travel coaching available across the USA and worldwide.


What makes Empire Boxing different is simple: every session is private, one-on-one. You won't be standing around in a class of 20–30 people waiting for attention. From the moment you walk in, the focus is on you — your technique, your footwork, your progress. That's how real boxing is taught.


Whether you're a parent looking for a safe, structured program for your child, an adult who's never thrown a punch, or an experienced fighter preparing for competition — Coach Francisco builds a training plan around your specific goals. No cookie-cutter workouts. No wasted time. Just real coaching from someone who has lived this sport at every level.


Empire Boxing is located in Plano, Texas and proudly serves the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. We're open 7 days a week with flexible scheduling to fit your life.
